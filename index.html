🧠 Compression Critique – Step 1

An independent scientific project, open to all

📄 A first document is already available:
Compression Critique v0.1 – Zenodo (official DOI)

It introduces a new way of formulating certain physical foundations,
seeking to bridge gravity and quantum mechanics through one simple principle:
make reality more understandable with fewer assumptions.

This is not a manifesto or speculation.
It’s a formal, testable foundation already shared publicly.

🔭 What this enables

  • YazChain – a lightweight, logic-regulated blockchain, with no energy waste
  • YazLang – a clear, simple programming language inspired by living systems
  • AI Applications – memory, perception, decision-making (details not disclosed yet)
  • Human body modeling – physiotherapy, muscular tension, movement
  • A new approach to research – open, sober, accessible

These applications are under development.
Some will soon be testable, others progressively shared.

🔐 What remains protected

It addresses the limits of v0.1 and provides a more complete framework.
But it won’t be released publicly yet — by choice, and for good reason.
